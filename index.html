Optimize Your Diet

  • Eat more leafy greens and cruciferous vegetables
  • Prioritize omega-3 fatty acids (fatty fish, flaxseeds)
  • Reduce processed sugars and refined carbs
  • Choose whole grains over white bread/pasta
  • Limit red meat and processed meats
  • Incorporate berries and colorful fruits
  • Use olive oil as your primary fat
  • Add nuts and seeds to your meals
  • Stay hydrated with water and herbal teas
  • Try intermittent fasting or time-restricted eating

Maximize Your Health Habits

  • Aim for 150+ minutes of moderate exercise weekly
  • Include strength training 2-3x/week
  • Prioritize sleep (7-9 hours/night)
  • Manage stress with meditation or deep breathing
  • Limit sitting; take movement breaks hourly
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol
  • Build social connections and community
  • Practice good dental hygiene
  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Get regular health screenings
